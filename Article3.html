Physics: Essential Formulas

A collection of well-known simple Physics formulas.

1. Motion & Forces

1. Speed

Speed = Distance / Time

Speed tells us how fast an object is moving.

2. Velocity

Velocity = Displacement / Time

Velocity is speed in a given direction.

3. Acceleration

a = (v − u) / t

Acceleration is the rate of change of velocity.

4. Newton’s Second Law

F = ma

The force on an object depends on its mass and acceleration.

5. Momentum

p = mv

Momentum describes the quantity of motion of an object.

2. Energy, Work & Power

6. Work Done

W = Fs

Work is done when a force moves an object.

7. Kinetic Energy

KE = ½mv²

Energy possessed by a moving object.

8. Gravitational Potential Energy

GPE = mgh

Energy stored due to an object’s height.

9. Power

P = W / t

Power is the rate of doing work.

3. Pressure & Density

10. Density

ρ = m / V

Density is mass per unit volume.

11. Pressure

P = F / A

Pressure is force acting per unit area.

4. Electricity

12. Electric Current

I = Q / t

Current is the rate of flow of charge.

13. Potential Difference

V = W / Q

Voltage is energy transferred per unit charge.

14. Ohm’s Law

V = IR

Shows the relationship between voltage, current, and resistance.

15. Electrical Power

P = VI

Power transferred in an electrical circuit.

5. Waves & Light

16. Wave Speed

v = fλ

Wave speed depends on frequency and wavelength.

17. Period of a Wave

T = 1 / f

The time taken for one complete oscillation.

6. Thermal Physics

18. Heat Energy

Q = mcΔT

Heat required to change the temperature of a substance.

7. Modern Physics

19. Energy–Mass Equation

E = mc²

Mass is a form of energy.

20. Efficiency

Efficiency = (Useful Output / Total Input) × 100%

Shows how effectively energy is used.
